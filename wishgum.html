Wishgum

Gift registry maker for weddings, birthdays etc.

Year

2021

Role

Product Designer & Frontend Developer

Platform

Web

Team

Founder, engineers, designer

Work done

Designed and developed a comprehensive gift registry platform that enables users to create personalized wishlists for special occasions like weddings, birthdays, and celebrations, complete with sharing functionality and gift purchasing workflows.

Context

Gift-giving for special occasions often involves guesswork and uncertainty about what recipients actually want. Wishgum was designed to solve this problem by creating an easy-to-use platform where people can create detailed gift registries for their important life events. The challenge was to create a platform that felt celebratory and personal while also being practical for gift-givers, with features that encourage meaningful gift-giving experiences.

Key Features

Designed and iterated on key features integral to setting up a gift registry and getting cash gifts.

Wishgum landing page

Sign in page

Modal to make a wish and encourage experience gifts as well as physical gifts

Dashbord with page visits and gifts received analytics

Wishlist management page - users can delete, add and reorder wishes

Wedding wishlist page sample

Making contributions to gift items

Gift checkout page

Design Challenges

  • Balancing celebratory aesthetics with practical functionality
  • Creating intuitive gift coordination without complex workflows
  • Designing for both registry creators and gift-givers equally
  • Managing privacy settings for personal gift preferences
  • Ensuring smooth mobile experience for quick gift browsing

Outcome

  • Successfully launched platform used for thousands of celebrations
  • Positive user feedback on ease of registry creation and sharing
  • Reduced gift-giving stress for both registry creators and gift-givers
  • High completion rates for gift registry workflows
  • Strong mobile engagement for gift browsing and purchasing
