Safety Centre

Safety education and information centre on a marketplace

Year

2023

Role

Product Designer

Platform

Web, Mobile

Team

PM, FE engineer, Designer

Work done

Designed a comprehensive safety education hub for marketplace users, creating educational resources, safety guidelines, and interactive learning modules to promote safe trading practices.

Context

Marketplaces face challenges with user safety, fraud, and trust issues. The Safety Centre was designed as a centralised hub where users can learn about safe trading practices, understand platform policies, and access resources to protect themselves from scams and fraudulent activities. The goal was to create an accessible, educational resource that empowers users with knowledge and tools to buy and sell safely while building trust in the marketplace ecosystem.

Process

Safety Centre homepage

  • User research - Understanding common safety concerns and fraud patterns
  • Content audit - Reviewing existing safety policies and identifying gaps
  • Information architecture - Organizing safety content for easy discovery
  • User journey mapping - Designing pathways for different safety scenarios
  • Interactive design - Creating engaging educational modules and assessments
  • Testing and iteration - Validating content clarity and user comprehension

Outcome

  • Increased user awareness of safety practices
  • Reduced fraud reports and safety incidents
  • Improved user trust and confidence in the platform
  • Enhanced marketplace reputation for safety and security
