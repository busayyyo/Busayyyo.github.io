Year

2021

Role

Product Designer

Platform

Web

Team

Founders, engineers, designer

Work done

Led product design from conception to implementation, including user research with Nigerian small businesses, prototyping e-commerce solutions, and designing intuitive shop creation and order management tools.

Context

Vendroad helps independent sellers create online shops, process orders, and handle payments. The challenge was designing a solution that could enable small businesses and independent sellers to easily create an online presence and manage their operations. The goal was to create an accessible platform tailored specifically to entrepreneurs who often operate solo without dedicated staff for digital tools.
