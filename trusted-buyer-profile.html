Trusted Buyer Profile

Exploring what makes a profile trustworthy in a marketplace

Year

2023

Role

UX Researcher & Product Designer

Platform

Web, Mobile

Team

PM, designer

Work done

Conducted comprehensive research to understand what makes buyer profiles trustworthy from a seller's perspective, leading to the design of trust indicators and profile enhancement features that increase transaction success rates.

Context

In peer-to-peer marketplaces, sellers often hesitate to engage with buyers who have incomplete or suspicious profiles. This research project aimed to identify the key factors that make a buyer appear trustworthy and legitimate, then translate those insights into actionable profile recommendations and trust-building features. The goal was to reduce seller anxiety, increase response rates, and ultimately facilitate more successful transactions between buyers and sellers.

User research findings and trust indicator prototypes

Research Process

  • Seller interviews - Understanding concerns and red flags when evaluating buyers
  • Profile analysis - Studying successful vs unsuccessful buyer interactions
  • Behavioral data analysis - Identifying patterns in high-converting buyer profiles
  • Trust signal testing - Rapid testing profiles with different completion rate with sellers
  • Cross-platform benchmarking - Analyzing trust indicators on other marketplaces

Key Findings

  • Profile photos significantly increase seller confidence and response rates
  • Verified contact information serves as a strong trust signal
  • Transaction history and positive feedback create credibility
  • Detailed, personal messages outperform generic inquiries
  • Quick response times indicate serious buyer intent
  • Social proof through mutual connections builds trust

Design Solutions

  • Progressive profile completion with trust score indicators
  • Verification badge system for phone, email, and identity
  • Guided messaging templates for first-time buyers
  • Social proof integration showing mutual connections
  • Trust-building tips and recommendations for new users
  • Seller confidence indicators based on buyer profile completeness

Outcome

  • Understanding of trust signals to prioritise and user concerns regarding data protection
  • Learnings were used in other departments across the platform to increase trust and user satisfaction
