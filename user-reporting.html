User Reporting

A channel to report bad actors on a marketplace

Year

2022

Role

Product Designer

Platform

Web, Mobile

Team

PM, engineers, designer

Work done

Designed a comprehensive user reporting system that enables users to easily report suspicious behavior, fraud, and policy violations while ensuring efficient processing and resolution by the trust & safety team.

Context

Online marketplaces face ongoing challenges with fraudulent users, scammers, and those who violate community guidelines. The User Reporting feature was designed to empower the community to self-moderate while providing trust & safety team with the tools and information needed to take swift action against bad actors.

User report modal

Process

  • Stakeholder interviews - Understanding customer service response to fraud and safety related issues
  • Report categorization - Mapping different types of violations and required evidence
  • Feedback loop design - Ensuring users receive appropriate follow-up communication

Key Features

Report trigger in chat

Categorised violation types

Immediate blocking on behalf of user when report happens (User can still choose to unblock)

Progress tracking and status updates for reporters

Mobile design

Outcome

  • Increased user reporting by 150% with clearer reporting paths
  • Reduced moderation response times through better categorization
  • Decreased repeat violations through effective enforcement
  • Improved user confidence in platform safety measures
  • Enhanced community self-regulation and collective responsibility
